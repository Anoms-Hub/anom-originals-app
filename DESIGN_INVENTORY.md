## Design Extraction & Enhancement Plan

### Phase 1: Extract Individual Designs
- Extract each design from composite sheets
- Remove text labels (will add product-specific text)
- Clean backgrounds for transparency
- Standardize dimensions

### Phase 2: Redesign & Enhance
- Increase resolution and quality
- Enhance colors and vibrancy
- Add premium effects (glow, shadow, depth)
- Optimize for merchandise printing

### Phase 3: Product Variants
Create 6 product types per design:
1. T-Shirt (front chest print, large)
2. Hoodie (front chest print, medium)
3. Mug (wrap-around print, medium)
4. Water Bottle (vertical print, small)
5. Coffee To-Go Bottle (vertical print, small)
6. Phone Case (square print, small)

### Phase 4: Upload & Catalog
- Upload all variants to CDN
- Create product database entries
- Set competitive pricing
- Integrate with Printful API
